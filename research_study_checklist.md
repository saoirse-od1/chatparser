# Research Study Checklist

## Title

- [ ] 1. Is my RSR written in Arial, size 12, 1.5 line spacing?
- [ ] 2. Have I written down the full title of my research study in the booklet?
- [ ] 3. Have I included the date parameters of my research study in the title?
- [ ] 4. Are the dates of my research study located in the period before 1993 if my subject relates to Irish history or before 1992 if my subject relates to the history of Europe and the wider world?

## Outline Plan

- [ ] 5. Have I written the heading "Define and justify"?
- [ ] 6. Have I explained what my study is about (define)?
- [ ] 7. Have I explained why my subject matters (justify)?
- [ ] 8. Have I written the heading "Aims"?
- [ ] 9. Have I given 3 aims about my topic that I hope to find the answers to?
- [ ] 10. Have I written the heading "Intended approach"?
- [ ] 11. Have I written a short paragraph about how I will research this project?
- [ ] 12. Cite at least two sources at Ordinary level or at least three at Higher level.
- [ ] 13. Have I cited my sources fully and accurately? (e.g. author, title, publisher, place of publication, year of publication)
- [ ] 14. Have I cited any website source that I used fully and accurately with full URL and date accessed?
- [ ] 15. Have I completed my Outline Plan on the correct page of the booklet?

## The Evaluation of the Sources

- [ ] 16. Have I evaluated each of the sources that I cited fully in my Outline Plan in the same order?
- [ ] 17. Have I commented on the relevance of each source to the subject of my study?
- [ ] 18. Have I commented on the strengths and weaknesses of each source to the subject of my study?
- [ ] 19. Have I given specific references to aspects of each source e.g. a map on Px, an image of Y under a specific tab of the website, difficult words used such as Z etc.
- [ ] 20. Have I completed the Evaluation of the Sources on the relevant pages of the booklet? Have I filled both pages?

## The Extended Essay

- [ ] 21. Have I included an introduction, paragraphs, and a conclusion?
- [ ] 22. Have I addressed in my essay the aims that I set down in my Outline Plan?
- [ ] 23. Have I ensured that the essay is sufficiently focused on the title of my study?
- [ ] 24. Have I used endnotes and listed them at the end of the essay?
- [ ] 25. Have I made sure my project makes sense, e.g. is it written in chronological order?
- [ ] 26. Have I included at least 3 quotes or references to sources/authors?
- [ ] 27. Have I ensured that my Extended Essay consists of 1200-1500 words (HL) / 600-800 words (OL), including my review?
- [ ] 28. Have I listed any other sources that I used at the end of my project?

## Review of the Research Process

- [ ] 29. Have I written a separate heading "Review of the research process"?
- [ ] 30. Have I included a review of the research process?

## Finally

- [ ] 31. Have I kept notes and rough drafts prepared as part of the research study, including tapes and/or transcripts of oral history testimony (where appropriate)?
- [ ] 32. Have I submitted my report to my teacher and signed the appropriate form?
